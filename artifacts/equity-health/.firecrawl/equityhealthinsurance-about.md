## WHO   WE   ARE?

### Best Private Health Insurance in Ghana

[Equity Health Insurance Ghana](https://www.facebook.com/TheEquityHealthInsurance) (EHI) is a trusted [**private health insurance provider in Ghana**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/), registered as a limited liability company and licensed under Act 852 of the National Health Insurance Authority (2012). We are committed to delivering comprehensive, reliable and [**affordable health insurance in Ghana**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/) tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals, families, and corporate organizations.

As one of the [**best health insurance companies in Ghana**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/), we work with a wide network of accredited service providers nationwide across [Ghana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghana). Our partners include hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, dental care centers, laboratories, diagnostic facilities, and optical centers, ensuring that our clients can access quality healthcare wherever they are.

Our [**health insurance plans**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/) are designed to fit every budget and medical need. From [**family and personal health plans in Ghana**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/ebusua-health-package/), [**personal health insurance**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/), [**corporate medical insurance**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/equity-corporate-package/), or [**travel health insurance**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/equity-international-health-package/). We cover both in-patient and out-patient services.

These include hospital consultations, surgeries, maternity care, dental treatment, prescribed medications, and optical services.

At [Equity Health Insurance Ghana](https://www.instagram.com/equityhealthinsurance/), customer satisfaction is central to everything we do. Our mission is to make [**private healthcare insurance**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/) accessible and affordable for everyone, central to everything we do.

Whether you need [**local medical insurance**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/) or [**international travel health insurance**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/), our dedicated team and nationwide network are here to safeguard your well-being.

With [Equity Health Insurance Ghana](https://www.youtube.com/@equityhealthinsurance8394), you are not just buying a policy, you are choosing [**the best private health insurance**](https://www.equityhealthinsurance.com/) partner you can trust.
